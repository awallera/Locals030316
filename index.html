Become Locals

Become Locals is an app that connects individuals in an area to specific events, activities, restaurants, and volunteer opportunities. 

The App

Routine, mundane activites got the best of us and we decided to do something about it. Locals caters to the adventourous soul in all of us, and tranforms the outsider into a friendly local.

We are group of young students looking to change the way college students and people in general live their lives by promoting and providing access to top tier, often unkown, local activites.

Let's get out, get active and become locals.
